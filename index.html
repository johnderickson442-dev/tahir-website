About Our Company

TAHIR TRADING COMPANY LTD

Based in the commercial hub of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, Tahir Trading Company Ltd is a premier trading and import-export enterprise dedicated to facilitating seamless international trade.

With our strategic location on Shekilango Road and deep understanding of both local and international markets, we serve as a reliable bridge between Tanzania and global trading partners, including esteemed relationships with diplomatic missions and international organizations.

Our commitment to excellence, transparent dealings, and professional integrity has established us as a trusted name in the Tanzanian business community.
